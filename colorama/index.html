Privacy Policy - Colorama

Effective date: March 22, 2026

Lynxora ("we", "us", or "our") built the Colorama app as a free, ad-supported application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Colorama does not require registration and does not ask you for any personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect certain data automatically:

  • Google AdMob - displays advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Firebase Analytics - helps us understand how users interact with the app (e.g., which coloring pages are most popular, how often the app is opened). This data is anonymous and aggregated.
  • Firebase Crashlytics - collects crash reports and diagnostic data to help us fix bugs and improve app stability. This may include device model, OS version, and stack traces.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected through third-party services is used solely to:

  • Display advertisements to support the free app
  • Understand app usage patterns to improve the user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues and crashes

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to any third parties beyond the services listed above.

3. Advertising

Colorama displays banner ads and rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. You may see personalized ads based on your interests or non-personalized ads depending on your device settings and consent choices.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

For more information on how Google handles data in ads, please visit: Google Ads Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage

Your coloring progress (which areas you have colored) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

5. Children's Privacy

Colorama is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal data input. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
